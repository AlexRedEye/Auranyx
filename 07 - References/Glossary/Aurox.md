**Pronunciation:** _awr-ox_ (OR-ox)
**(noun)**  
The scientific and technical term for ambient mana: a naturally occurring energy field produced by deep planetary processes.  
Aurox rises through vents, travels along ley lines, and accumulates in regions with favorable geological or celestial conditions. Measurable, quantifiable, and subject to ecological balance, Aurox fuels all biological magic through interaction with Aurynea.

**Usage:**  
“Aurox density dropped by 17%—prepare for weakened casting conditions.”
